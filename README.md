# 🎨 Single Color Memory Game

**How well can you remember a color… with nothing but your brain?**

Welcome to a minimalist yet addictive color challenge inspired by Coloruno — where your perception, memory, and attention to detail are put to the ultimate test.

---

## 🧠 The Challenge

You’ll be shown **one color** for just a few seconds.

No hints. No second chances.

Then it disappears.

Your mission?
🎯 **Recreate that exact color from memory.**

Sounds easy… until you try it.

---

## 🎮 How It Works

1. **👀 Observe**
   A random color flashes on your screen (3–5 seconds)

2. **🧠 Memorize**
   Focus on:

   * Hue (the actual color)
   * Saturation (how intense it is)
   * Brightness (light vs dark)

3. **🎛 Recreate**
   Use sliders to adjust:

   * Hue (0–360°)
   * Saturation (0–100%)
   * Brightness (0–100%)

4. **📊 Get Scored**
   Your guess is compared with the original color
   → You get a **score from 0 to 100%**

## 🔥 Difficulty Progression

Think you’ve mastered it? Try:

* ⏱ Shorter display times

## ⚙️ Scoring System

Your accuracy is calculated based on:

* Hue difference (most important 👀)
* Saturation difference
* Brightness difference

Smarter than simple RGB — it’s designed to match **how humans actually see color**.
